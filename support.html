Frequently Asked Questions

Why is a service not loading?

Check your internet connection and press ⌘R to reload. Some services block generic web access — try using the direct web URL (e.g. web.whatsapp.com instead of whatsapp.com).

I got logged out after a restart

This can happen after a macOS update clears app data containers. Log back in — your account list (name, URL, icon) is safely stored in exHub's settings; only the web session was cleared by macOS.

Notifications are not appearing

Open System Settings → Notifications → exHub and ensure Allow Notifications is on. Also check Account Manager to confirm Notifications is toggled on for the account. Notifications require exHub Pro.

How do I restore my Pro subscription?

Open Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases and sign in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

How do I delete an account?

Right-click the account row in the sidebar and choose Delete, or open Account Manager, select the account, and click the Delete button.
