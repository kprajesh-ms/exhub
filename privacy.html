Overview

exHub is a macOS app that lets you access multiple web-based messaging services in isolated sessions. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data exHub handles and how.

Data We Do Not Collect

exHub does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. There is no backend, no analytics, and no telemetry. We never see your messages, credentials, or activity.

Data Stored Locally on Your Mac

  • Account list — the names, URLs, and icon choices you set up are stored in macOS User Defaults on your device only.
  • Web session data — cookies, local storage, and cache for each account are stored by the macOS WebKit engine inside the app's sandboxed data container on your device.

None of this data leaves your Mac.

Logo and Favicon Fetching

When you add a service with the "Auto" icon setting, exHub makes a one-time network request to Clearbit Logo API (logo.clearbit.com) and Google Favicon Service to retrieve the service's brand logo. Only the domain name is sent in this request — no personal information is included.

In-App Purchases

exHub Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

App Sandbox

exHub runs in the macOS App Sandbox. It can only access your Downloads folder (to save files you download) and the network (to load web services). It cannot access your contacts, other files, or other applications.

Children's Privacy

exHub is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of exHub after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
